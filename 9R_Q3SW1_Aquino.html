Mob Psycho 100 (Season 1)

᲼


~ Synopsis ~

᲼

Mob Psycho 100 is an anime about a boy, Shigeo "Mob" Kageyama, who owns telekinetic powers powerful to destroy an entire city with a thought. Since he started middle school, he slowly got more bored of his powers, and wishes to live a normal day-to-day life. Having difficulty to accomplish this, he encounters multiple people who can help him.
᲼

᲼


* Major Characters *


᲼



᲼

Reigen Arataka

Reigen Arataka is introduced along with Mob from the very beginning of the series. He is well-known for his exorcism scams that easily drain the pockets of a single customer. Despite this, he accompanies Shigeo quite often for the duration of the first season alone, hence Mob acknowledges him as his "Master."

᲼

᲼

Shigeo "Mob" Kageyama

Kageyama , also known as Mob , is a 14 year-old boy who has psychic powers. Despite using these to benefit himself in his daily life, he rarely uses it in most cases so he can experience what it's like to live normally.
᲼

Dimple

Dimple introduces himself as antagonist on his first encounter with Mob, only to switch sides after being defeated. Afterwards, he eventually became of Shigeo's acquaintance, assisting him throughout the entire series.

᲼


* Minor Characters *


᲼



᲼

Ritsu Kageyama

Ritsu is Mob's younger brother. When often in Mob's presence, he always tries helps him out as a brother. However, as he lives with him, he becomes more and more jealous of Shigeo's powers.

᲼

᲼

Tsubomi Takane

Tsubomi is Mob's love interest. The first time they had met was when they were young, where Mob already liked her. As a result, he would try to impress Tsubomi with his powers, but it became a bore to her as years passed.
᲼

Teruki Hanazawa

ALso known as Teru , Hanazawa was first introduced as an evil character. He is another known psychic who utilizes his powers for speed. To his unfortune, he was also defeated by Mob, similar to how Dimple was defeated.
